Bacon and Brie Fettuccine

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
bacon, chopped 1⁄2 lb
half-and-half 1⁄2 cup
canned low sodium chicken broth 1⁄2 cup
freshly grated parmesan cheese 1 cup
cracked black pepper 1 teaspoon
brie cheese, rind removed,thinly sliced 3 ounces
dry fettuccine 1 lb

Step 1: Cook bacon in heavy large skillet over medium heat until fat is rendered and bacon begins to brown, about 6 minutes.
Step 2: Using slotted spoon, transfer bacon to plate.
Step 3: Discard fat from skillet.
Step 4: Add half and half and chicken broth to same skillet and bring to boil over medium heat.
Step 5: Gradually add Parmesan and stir until cheese melts.
Step 6: Add pepper and bacon. Reduce heat to low and gradually add Brie, stirring until cheese melts.
Step 7: Meanwhile, cook fettuccine in large pot of boiling salted water until just tender but still firm to bite.
Step 8: Drain well. Add fettuccine to sauce in skillet and Toss until coated with sauce.
Step 9: Divide fettuccine among plates and serve.
