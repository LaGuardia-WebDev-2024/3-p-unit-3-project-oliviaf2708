Bacon and Brie Fettuccine

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
bacon, chopped 1/2 lb
half-and-half 1/2 cup
canned low sodium chicken broth 1/2 cup
freshly grated parmesan cheese 1 cup
cracked black pepper 1 teaspoon
brie cheese, rind removed,thinly sliced 3 ounces
dry fettuccine 1 lb

Step 1: Cook bacon in heavy large skillet over medium heat until fat is rendered and bacon begins to brown, about 6 minutes.
Step 2: Using slotted spoon, transfer bacon to plate.
Step 3: Discard fat from skillet.
Step 4: Add half and half and chicken broth to same skillet and bring to boil over medium heat.
Step 5: Gradually add Parmesan and stir until cheese melts.
Step 6: Add pepper and bacon. Reduce heat to low and gradually add Brie, stirring until cheese melts.
Step 7: Meanwhile, cook fettuccine in large pot of boiling salted water until just tender but still firm to bite.
Step 8: Drain well. Add fettuccine to sauce in skillet and Toss until coated with sauce.
Step 9: Divide fettuccine among plates and serve.

Pistachio Pesto Pasta

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
fresh basil leaves 1 heaping cup
toasted shelled pistachios 2 tbsp
fresh lemon juice 1 tbsp
garlic 1 clove
diced cherry tomatoes 1 cup
fresh spinach or arugula 1 large handful
penne pasta 8 oz
halved or quartered artichoke hearts 1 cup
nutritional yeast 1/2 tbsp
red pepper flakes 1/4 teaspoon
salt 1/4 tsp
pepper a pinch
extra virgin olive oil 2 tbsp

Step 1: To make your pistachio pesto, add your fresh basil, toasted pistachio pieces, fresh lemon juice, garlic, nutritional yeast, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (optional). Pulse until combined but not completely smooth, scraping down a few times in between.
Step 2: Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while pulsing until combined and desired texture is achieved.
Step 3: In a large pot, boil water with a pinch of salt. Add you penne and cook until al dente. Drain and rinse your cooked pasta and add it back to the pot.
Step 4: Add your pistachio pesto, artichokes, cherry tomato halves, and greens. Stir until combined and warm over medium heat for a few minutes.
Step 5: Serve your pistachio pesto pasta topped with more pistachio pieces, nutritional yeast/parmesan, and/or red pepper flakes.
Step 6: TOAST YOUR PISTACHIOS: To toast your shelled pistachios, spread them evenly on a baking sheet, bake at 350°F for about 7-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they’re golden and aromatic. *Watch them closely so they don’t burn.
